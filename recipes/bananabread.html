Banana Bread

You see those sad, discolored shadow of their former selves bunch of bananas on your counter? Turn their frown upside down!

Give them a new life in your belly in the form of banana bread!

Ingredients
  • All of the bananas on your counter that are past their prime, mushed in a bowl
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Enough cinnamon that you feel the breath of your ancestors on your neck as they urgently whisper it's too much, my child
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • a lil more cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • You know you probably didn't add enough cinnamon, but you've run out. again. it's ok
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Grease a loaf pain
  3. Melt stick of butter
  4. Add bananas and mash together
  5. Add the vanilla extract and egg to the bowl and use the same fork to mash and stir until no yellow streaks of egg remain
  6. In a separate bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon
  7. Gradually add to bowl with banana mixture and stir until just combined
  8. Pour into prepared loaf pan and bake for 45-55 minutes until a toothpick (steak knife in my house) comes out clean
  9. Enjoy still warm with a small pat of butter *chef's kiss*
