Apple Pie

What is truly better than a warm apple pie with hints of nutmeg and cinnamon (okay more than a hint of cinnamon), and a touch of nostalgia?

Tart and sweet, all in one bite.

Ingredients
  • Premade pie crust (homemade or store-bought)
  • 3 cups of apples peeled and sliced
  • splash of lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Enough cinnamon that you start to question your sanity
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Okay more cinnamon, that wasn't enough, I promise
  • Unsalted butter, about 2 tbsp
  • You know you probably didn't add enough cinnamon, but you've run out. it's ok
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°
  2. In a pie plate, unroll pie crust and press firmly against sides
  3. In a bowl, add peeled and sliced apples and splash of lemon juice
  4. Add apples on top of pie crust
  5. Sprinkle sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, flour, and more cinnamon on top of apples
  6. Cut butter into small cubes and dot on top of dry ingredients
  7. Top with second pie crust, and crimp edges
  8. Cut slits in top of crust in whatever design makes you happy
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 40-45 minutes
