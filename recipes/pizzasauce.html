Pizza Sauce

This sauce does it all--Pizza, Spaghetti, Meatball sanniches; what's not to love?!

Mercifully, if you've seen my other recipes, you can hold the cinnamon.

Ingredients
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • As many cloves of garlic as you can stand peeling, minced (Jarlic is fine, but not as flavorful, so add some more)
  • A good handful of sugar
  • A good healthy shake of onion powder
  • A less healthy shake of garlic powder
  • About a palmful or a little less of salt
  • A good healthy shake of Italian seasoning
  • At least 3 good health shakes of parsley
  • A regular sized can of petite diced tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Don't be scared to add a lil more sugar
Steps
  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat and add the olive oil, just enough to coat the bottom
  2. Open the can of tomato paste and go ahead and put a spoon in it so you don't burn the garlic
  3. Saute the garlic in the olive oil and add the tomato paste before it burns
  4. Add the rest of the seasoning including the sugar
  5. Stir well and add the petite diced tomatoes
  6. Continue to stir until it starts to bubble then turn heat way low
  7. Stir occasionally while it gets to know itself in the pot
  8. Portion out and freeze, or use immediately in your red sauce dish of choice
